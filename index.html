SANS-SERIF TYPEFACE

Lacking serifs, this sans-serif typeface appears clean, modern and highly suitable for contemporary digital design.

2

BOLD STROKE

Ultra-thick strokes show minimal contrast and transitions.

3

HIGH X-HEIGHT

The typeface features an unusually tall x-height, where lowercase letters appear significantly larger in proportion to ascenders, creating a compact legible appearance that enhances readability, especially at smaller sizes or in digital contexts where clarity is essential.

4

TINY INTERIOR HEIGHT

The interior spaces of letters, like "o" and "a" are notably small, creating a dense, compact appearance that reduces openness.

FUN APPLICATION

Impact has been widely used for internet memes, often paired with white text and a black outline for dramatic effect. Despite its association with humor and memes, the font was designed for serious use in advertising and headlines. Its strong presence also made it popular in comic books and movie posters, where it added a sense of urgency or emphasis.

×

VERSATILITY

Impact is still highly relevant in today’s design world, especially in advertising, posters, and social media graphics. Its bold, clear-cut lines make it ideal for grabbing attention in crowded digital spaces. Whether on billboards or in memes, it continues to convey power, immediacy, and importance in modern communication.

×

EVOLUTION

Impact has adapted seamlessly to the digital age, despite its origins. Today, it remains a popular choice for headlines, call-to-action buttons, and social media posts. Its ability to stand out on both small screens and large formats keeps it relevant, especially in visual media that needs to grab attention fast.

×

TYPEFACE LIMIT

Impact isn't suitable for body text because its thick strokes and tight spacing make long passages hard to read, causing letters to blur together creating visual fatigue.
